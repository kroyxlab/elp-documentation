Encapsulation

We aim to create a language that encapsulates as much scientific and mathematical knowledge as possible.

Intuitiveness

We aim to create an intuitive understanding of the world around us from a mathematical and scientific perspective.

Evolution

We aim to raise a generation of native speakers to be tomorrow's leaders.
